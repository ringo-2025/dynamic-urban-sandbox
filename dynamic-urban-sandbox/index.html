About This Sandbox

This Dynamic Urban Sandbox enables policymakers to test policy effectiveness and assess their impact on Hong Kong's future urban development. By simulating citizen responses through 1000 AI agents, it provides valuable insights for urban planning decisions that enhance livability for Hong Kong residents.
